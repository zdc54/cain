- Press "Enter Fastboot (MTK)"

- Insert the cable

- Quickly connect to MTK preloader port once it appears in the browser USB menu

Once you're in Fastboot mode, do the following:

- Press "Jailbreak"

- Connect to "Android" port once it appears in the browser USB menu

If the flashing doesn't succeed:

- Reconnect in Fastboot mode

- Press "Attempt OEM unlock"

- Repeat the whole procedure
